Create an app at https://instagram.com/developer/clients/manage/

Add website as a platorm with {{ request.scheme }}://{{ request.get_host }}/ as the website and add {{ request.scheme }}://{{ request.get_host }}{% url 'dispatch_agent_config_url' agent_config_id=object.pk view_name=object.agent.ACTION_CONFIG_KEYS.access_token %} as an Redirect URI.

Then enter the App id(client id) and App secret(client secret) bellow. Then click the Refresh button next to Access token to connect to your Instagram account.
